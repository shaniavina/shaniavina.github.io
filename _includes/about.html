Han Fang

Han Fang is a PhD candidate in Applied Math and Statistics at Stony Brook University , in residence at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory . He received his B.S. in Optical Informatics at Sun Yat-sen University. He is currently supervised by Dr. Michael Schatz and Dr. Gholson Lyon. His research interests intersect at machine learning, statistics, and genomics, with the goal of understanding underlying patterns of complex data.
